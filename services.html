The Full Offering

01 — The Flagship

Brand Films

BF

This is where we do our most important work. A brand film isn't a product showcase — it's a portrait. It answers the question every audience is quietly asking: who are you, really?

We focus on emotion, identity, and mood. The goal isn't to sell — it's to make people feel the brand in a way that advertising alone never can. When done right, a brand film becomes an asset that works for years.

Story-driven narrative Emotion-first direction 1–5 mins Cinematic grade Sound design
02 — The Fast Frame

Reels / Social Videos

RS

Short doesn't mean shallow. The best reels are compressed cinema — every cut earns its place, every second is accountable.

We create reels that grab attention in the first three seconds and hold it through deliberate pacing and visual tension. Designed for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts — built to stop the scroll and start the conversation.

15–60 seconds Hook-first structure Vertical 9:16 Trend-aware Quick turnaround
03 — The Memory

Event Aftermovies

EA

An event lasts a night. An aftermovie lasts forever. We don't just record what happened — we reconstruct the feeling of being there.

Energy, atmosphere, the raw emotion of a crowd, the quiet between the moments — all of it captured and shaped into a cinematic recap that makes those who attended relive it, and those who didn't wish they'd been there. A good event aftermovie is the best marketing you'll ever have.

Cinematic coverage Atmosphere-focused Sound design Event highlight Multi-day events
04 — The Persuader

Commercial Ads

CA

Promotional, yes. But never generic. A commercial ad still needs to make the viewer feel something — that's the difference between an ad that works and one that gets skipped.

We create commercial content that communicates key messages with clarity and visual confidence. Built to drive engagement, build brand awareness, and influence perception — through storytelling, not just product shots.

Product-focused Message clarity Brand awareness Paid media ready Strategic direction
05 — The Pulse

Music Visuals

MV

Music already moves. Our job is to give it a face — to build a visual world that amplifies what the sound has already started.

We create music videos and visual content for artists and musicians that bring the mood and emotion of a track to life through cinematic imagery. The result is a visual identity for the song — something the listener returns to because it deepens what they already felt.

Artist identity Mood-driven imagery Narrative or abstract Music-synced edit Lyric visuals
06 — The Truth

Documentary

DC

Some stories can't be told in 60 seconds. Some truths need room to breathe — time to unfold — without shortcuts.

Documentary work at The 27th Frame means patient storytelling. Real subjects, genuine emotion, and a commitment to showing what actually happened — not a polished version of it. Whether it's a brand's journey, a community's story, or an artist's process, we approach it with the same cinema discipline.

Behind the Lens

How a Project
Comes to Life

01

Pre-Production

Idea development, concept writing, scripting, shot lists, mood boards, and a deep client conversation. Nothing goes in front of a lens until we know exactly what it needs to say.

02

Production

On the day, we direct with purpose. Every shot is chosen. Every angle is considered. We don't shoot and hope — we execute what we planned and adapt when something real and better presents itself.

03

Post-Production

Editing that serves the story, color grading that creates mood, sound design that elevates feeling. This is where cinema is made — not on set, but in the hours spent making every second count.

✦

The Final Frame

Delivery isn't the end. We stay present. We want to know if it worked — because our goal isn't just to hand over a file. It's to have created something that actually moved the needle.
